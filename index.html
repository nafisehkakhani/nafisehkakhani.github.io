Nafiseh Kakhani


I am a Remote Sensing Engineer and Geospatial Data Scientist, currently a Postdoc Researcher at the University of Tuebingen (Germany) in the Geoscience department under Prof. Dr. Thomas Scholten. My area of expertise lies in using computer vision for earth observation, but lately, I've been focusing on applying machine learning and deep learning to extract environmental insights, especially concerning soil. My academic background includes a Bachelor's in Geomatics Engineering, followed by a Master's and PhD in Remote Sensing.
